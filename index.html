Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

Myrix Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile game Myrix (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or contacts.

b) Non-Personal / Game Data

The App stores limited gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • Current level
  • Game progress
  • Gameplay statistics

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

c) Data Collected by Third-Party Services

While the App itself does not directly collect personal data, certain third-party services may be used in current or future versions of the App, as described below.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App may include advertisements in current or future versions using Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google LLC.

If advertising is enabled in a version of the App, Google AdMob may automatically collect and process certain information, including but not limited to:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App usage and interaction data

This information may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements, depending on user settings, consent, and applicable regulations.

The developer does not directly collect, store, or control advertising data collected by third-party providers.

Google’s use of data is governed by its Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Analytics

The App does not currently use analytics services.

If analytics services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and such services will collect only anonymous or aggregated data where applicable.

4. Children’s Privacy

This App is suitable for general audiences.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If advertising is enabled in future versions, it will be implemented in compliance with applicable advertising and child protection regulations, including Google Play’s Families policies where applicable.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data.

Since the App operates primarily offline and stores gameplay data locally on your device, privacy risks are minimal.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services in current or future versions, including:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

7. Permissions

The App may request basic permissions such as audio playback and local storage access. These permissions are used solely for gameplay functionality and are not used to collect personal data.

8. Data Deletion

Since the App does not store personal data on external servers, users may delete all locally stored game data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App’s storage from device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the App from their device.

If you have any questions regarding data handling or deletion, please contact us using the details below.
