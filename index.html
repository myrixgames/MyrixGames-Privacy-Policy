Myrix Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile game Myrix (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, or contacts.

b) Non-Personal / Game Data

While the App itself does not collect personal data, third-party services used in the App may collect certain information as described below.

Google AdMob may automatically collect and process certain information, including but not limited to:

The developer does not directly collect, store, or control this advertising data.

Google’s use of data is governed by its Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The App does not currently use analytics services. If analytics are added in the future, they will collect only anonymous, aggregated usage data, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

This App is suitable for general audiences, including children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Ads shown via Google AdMob are served in compliance with Google’s policies for child-directed and family-friendly apps.
